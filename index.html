Race Notes & Architectures

  • Stock Ticker Note: Tickers are provided for publicly traded companies (**IBM**, **IONQ**, **QBTS**). **GOOGL** represents Google's parent company, Alphabet Inc. Companies marked **N/A** (Atom Computing, Quantinuum) are currently privately held.
  • Superconducting: The most mature approach, favored by IBM and Google. Requires extreme cooling.
  • Trapped Ion: Known for high qubit fidelity (quality), used by IonQ and Quantinuum.
  • Neutral Atoms: High scalability and large arrays, demonstrated by Atom Computing.
  • Quantum Annealing: Specializes in optimization problems, pioneered by D-Wave.
  • **Note:** Qubit counts are highly variable across architectures. This ranking is symbolic, prioritizing a mix of raw count and leading performance claims reported in late 2024/2025.
